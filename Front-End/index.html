About

Village-like appeal and nightlife at the heart of Brooklyn's Boerum Hill.

The property sits at the meeting place of two of Brooklyn's greatest and most convenient neighborhoods. Cobble Hill, known for its quiet streets, restaurants, and shopping, offers any resident everything needed for a full city life. Boerum Hill, known for its lively bars and relaxed streets of shops, offers immediate proximity to concerts and sporting events at Barclays Center as well as the offices and central transit of Downtown Brooklyn. It's everything you need, steps from Wyckoff Street.
